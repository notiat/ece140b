What We Do

CREATE VEHICLE SECURITY SOLUTIONS

Our product is an IOT device that protects your car from theft and vandalism. The device includes a sensor kit that will be attached to your car to detect any disturbances. These disturbances will be connected to your CarProtect app where it will notify you and the authorities while recording all the events going on at your vehicle.

The included features at CarProtect 1.0 are:

  • Patented TheftDetect Alarm
  • 360 degree smart camera
