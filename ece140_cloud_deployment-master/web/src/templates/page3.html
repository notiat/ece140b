Features and Interactions

Motion Sensor

The main feature of our device: alerts users when there is any unwanted foreign forces acting on the user's vehicle.

Internet Connectivity

Access the status of your vehicle's security from anywhere with an internet connection.

Compact Design

Clean, unintrusive design that is also easy to attach and set up.

Built-In Camera

Optional, built-in camera to act as a dash-cam and reduce clutter in your car with too many devices.

GPS Location

Real time position tracking for security, but also for remembering where you parked your car!

Key Product Interactions

Our key product interaction is the app interaction with the user. The app will have all the important information, and it will be the center of controlling CarProtect. It will be clear and concise with vehicle safety status, location and device status.
