Cost and Revenue

Cost

We estimate the total cost of manufacturing a single device is around $23.5. The raw materials and electrical components costs $10. Labor and manufacturing will cost another $10. Running the servers will be around $2 per device per month. Logistics of shipping the units is around $1.50 per unit.

Revenue

We plan to sell this product as a single purchase for $45 which includes hardware + SIM renewal + cost of running web servers. However, we are looking into several other models that may be better for the long run. Our current revenue model is a one time purchase from a retailer, where the user can easily install the unit on their own, and pays a small monthly fee for the service and the internet connection. Another revenue model we can look into is a subscription model with a deposit for the unit and a one time installation fee with a monthly subscription. Since there is a high barrier to entry for the pricing of other competing products, we plan to create a low barrier to entry for our device with a one month refund period.
